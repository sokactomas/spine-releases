# Spine v0.5.6

Released: 2026-07-02

## What's new

This release makes Spine's compounding edge — the reason a Spine user pulls ahead of a colleague on the same CLI — truthful and visible, with no extra work on your part.

- Your compounding edge, in view: the status bar now shows, quietly and always on, how much durable knowledge Spine carries for you across Claude and Codex, and how many tokens its local background work kept off your cloud quota. The longer you work, the further ahead of a plain terminal you are.
- Truthful token savings: local embedding work now counts toward saved tokens too, not just distillation — so the savings figure reflects everything Spine runs off your cloud quota instead of undercounting it.

## Install

1. Download the `Spine_0.5.6_universal.dmg` asset from GitHub Releases.
2. Open the DMG and drag Spine to Applications.
3. Launch Spine and restart your CLI if it was already running.
